Understanding Semantic HTML5

Published on February 1, 2026

What is Semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML uses meaningful tags to clearly describe the purpose of content, improving accessibility and SEO.

Why is it Important?

It helps screen readers, search engines, and developers understand the structure of a webpage.
